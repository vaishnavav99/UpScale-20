What is Upscale

UpScale is a event conducted with the aim of exposing participants to the multifaced dimensions of entrepreneurship with renewed focus on chiselling core competencies as well as the skillset required to be an entrepreneur.

  • Communication skills
  • Leadership skills
  • Critical thinking
  • Support launch Startups
  • Idea evaluation

Schedule

Spread over a span of 7 days, with each day tailored specifically for the participants to maximize their performance & aquire knowledge.

16-09-2020 - Meet & Greet

Explore Discord - Understand the features the platform provides and learn to utilise it.
Networking- Get to know your like-minded peers. Meet the people you'll be working with.




17-09-2020 - Flagging off

Inaugural ceremony - A short session to officially start the event and to explain about the rules of the event.
Business model and marketing- A webinar detailing crucial aspects of successful launching a business.



18-09-2020 – Pitch Day

60 Seconds to Pitch- The participants are given a minute to present their idea and take the first step towards realising it.
Customer development model - A webinar which provides insight into topics such as customer validation, MVP, Prototype, and pricing.


19-09-2020 – Take Notes

Learn from the Best - Local mentors will be coming in throughout the event to coach you through the hard problems.
How to Pitch an Idea? - Have a great idea but don't know how to present it? After this session you won't have to worry about it again.

20-09-2020 – Dry Run

Mock presentation - Conduct a mock presentation from what you've learnt so far.
Legal formalities for start-up and how to get funding -- A valuable session to learn about financial and legal aspects of a business venture.

21-09-2020 – The Real Deal

Present in Front of Panel Judges- You'll have 5 minutes to present the product or service you built to the crowd and a panel of experts.
Evaluation - The teams are evaluated on the basis of their performance throughout the week and a winner is declared.

22-09-2020- Conclusion

Closing ceremony - A small session evaluating the event and officially declaring the winner.
Celebrate - As the event winds up, time to kick back and start your venture.
